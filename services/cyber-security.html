FAQ

Common questions

QWhat is the difference between vulnerability assessment and penetration testing?+

A vulnerability assessment identifies and categorizes potential weaknesses. Penetration testing goes further by actively attempting to exploit those weaknesses to determine the actual risk they pose.

QHow often should we conduct a cyber security assessment?+

At minimum, once a year, and after significant changes such as new system deployment, mergers, or major infrastructure upgrades. Regulated industries may require more frequent assessments.

QDo you help with NCA compliance in Saudi Arabia?+

Yes. We help organizations align with NCA ECC, NCA CCC, and SAMA Cyber Security Framework requirements from gap assessment through remediation and audit readiness.

QCan you help us respond to an active cyber attack?+

Yes. Contact us immediately. Our incident response team can help contain the threat, assess damage, and begin recovery to minimize business impact.

QWe are a small business. Do we really need cyber security services?+

Yes. Small and mid-sized businesses are increasingly targeted because attackers know they often have weaker defenses. The consequences of a breach can be serious regardless of company size.
