From Blueprint to Live Network, Delivered on Time and Built to Perform

Planning a network is one thing. Deploying it successfully across multiple sites, within tight timelines, and without disrupting operations is another. Overview

Purpose-built technology for Saudi businesses

Network project deployment is where planning meets reality. Designs are tested, timelines are pressured, coordination challenges emerge, and the quality of your delivery partner becomes clear. Geagle brings a structured approach with project managers and network engineers working together from day one, ensuring what is designed is what gets built, and what gets built actually works.

FAQ

Common questions

QHow do you handle network deployments across multiple cities in Saudi Arabia?+

A central project manager coordinates all sites, standardized playbooks keep quality consistent, and regular reporting keeps stakeholders informed across locations.

QWhat happens if something goes wrong during cutover?+

Every cutover plan includes a rollback procedure. If something does not go as planned, we restore the previous network state while diagnosing and resolving the issue.

QDo you work with our existing vendors and equipment?+

Yes. We work with major vendors including Cisco, Aruba, Juniper, Fortinet, Ubiquiti, and others, whether equipment is supplied by Geagle or already owned by your organization.

QCan you deploy a network while our existing one is still running?+

Yes. We build and test the new network alongside the existing one, then execute a controlled cutover to minimize downtime and disruption.

QWhat documentation do we receive after the project?+

You receive a complete handover pack including as-built network diagrams, device configuration backups, cable schedules, test results, and a project completion report.
