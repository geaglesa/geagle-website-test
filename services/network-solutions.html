Overview

Purpose-built technology for Saudi businesses

Every digital service your business depends on, from cloud applications and video calls to security cameras and payment systems, runs on your network. What We Deliver

Focused capabilities for real business outcomes

Network Design and Architecture

Network Installation and Configuration

Wireless Network Solutions

LAN and WAN Setup and Management

Network Monitoring and Management

Network Upgrades and Scalability Planning

FAQ

Common questions

QHow long does a network installation take?+

A standard office network installation typically takes 2 to 5 days depending on size and complexity. Larger or multi-site deployments are planned in phases with minimal disruption.

QCan you upgrade our existing network without shutting everything down?+

Yes. We plan upgrades carefully to minimize downtime, often scheduling critical changes during off-hours or weekends so your team stays productive.

QDo you provide ongoing network support after installation?+

Yes. We offer managed network services including 24/7 monitoring, regular health checks, and priority support.

QWhat brands and vendors do you work with?+

We are experienced with Cisco, Aruba, Juniper, Fortinet, Ubiquiti, MikroTik, and several other leading vendors. Recommendations are based on requirements and budget.

QCan you connect multiple office branches?+

Yes. We design and manage WAN solutions using leased lines, MPLS, SD-WAN, or secure VPN tunnels to keep performance consistent across sites.
