Overview

Purpose-built technology for Saudi businesses

Enterprise infrastructure is different from small business IT. Scale is larger, complexity is greater, stakes are higher, and requirements for availability, security, performance, and compliance are more demanding. Geagle helps Saudi organizations modernize legacy systems, support digital transformation, align with NCA and SAMA expectations, and build infrastructure environments designed for enterprise demands.

FAQ

Common questions

QHow do you approach a large enterprise infrastructure project?+

Every engagement starts with assessment and a detailed project plan covering scope, design, timelines, resources, risks, and communication. Delivery is phased carefully with dependencies managed throughout.

QCan you work alongside our existing internal IT team?+

Yes. We regularly work as an extension of internal IT teams, adding specialist expertise and delivery capacity while adapting to your organization's structure.

QHow do you ensure high availability in enterprise infrastructure?+

High availability is designed from the start through redundant power, dual network paths, clustered compute, replicated storage, automated failover, and tested failure scenarios.

QDo you support hybrid infrastructure environments?+

Yes. We design and manage hybrid environments that combine on-premises infrastructure with public and private cloud while maintaining consistent security and management.

QHow do you handle NCA and SAMA compliance requirements for infrastructure?+

We assess your compliance posture, identify infrastructure gaps, and implement technical and procedural controls aligned with NCA ECC, NCA CCC, and SAMA Cyber Security Framework requirements.
