Physical & IT Security in Saudi Arabia

Security Is Not a Feature. It Is a Foundation.

Whether it is protecting your premises, your people, or your data, security cannot be an afterthought. Geagle delivers comprehensive security solutions covering both physical and digital layers, from CCTV and access control to network security and endpoint protection.

Overview

Purpose-built technology for Saudi businesses

Modern businesses face threats from multiple directions at once. A breach does not always come through a firewall, and a camera system alone will not stop a ransomware attack. Geagle takes a holistic approach, assessing vulnerabilities across physical and digital surfaces and implementing layered security solutions that work together across offices, industrial facilities, retail chains, healthcare institutions, and government buildings.

What We Deliver

Focused capabilities for real business outcomes

CCTV and Surveillance Systems

Professional IP camera, NVR/DVR, remote viewing, and intelligent video analytics systems planned through site surveys to reduce blind spots.

Access Control Systems

Electronic access control including key cards, biometrics, PIN entry, and visitor management for server rooms, warehouses, offices, and facilities.

Intrusion Detection and Alarm Systems

Motion sensors, door and window contacts, glass break detectors, alarm panels, monitoring integrations, and mobile alerts for unusual activity.

Firewall Deployment and Management

Next-generation firewall deployment, rule setup, traffic filtering, VPN configuration, application-layer inspection, ongoing rule review, and firewall audits.

Network Security

Next-generation firewalls, IPS, secure DNS, and network segmentation strategies that prevent unauthorized access and contain threats before they spread.

Endpoint Security

Endpoint protection, EDR tools, and device management policies that secure laptops, desktops, mobile devices, and servers across your environment.

Security Information and Event Management

SIEM deployment and management to centralize logs, correlate security events, detect threats, generate alerts, and support compliance reporting.

Data Loss Prevention

DLP policies and tools that help protect customer records, financial information, and intellectual property from unauthorized movement.

Security Awareness Training

Structured training programs that help staff recognize phishing, handle sensitive information, and follow practical security best practices.

Physical Security Consulting

Premises assessments covering access points, surveillance coverage, emergency response procedures, and prioritized security recommendations.

FAQ

Common questions

QDo you handle both installation and ongoing monitoring?+

Yes. We offer one-time installation projects and ongoing managed security services, including 24/7 monitoring, health checks, and rapid incident response.

QCan you integrate CCTV and access control with our existing systems?+

In most cases, yes. We assess existing infrastructure first and design integrations that maximize current investment before recommending new hardware.

QHow do you ensure cameras cover all areas without gaps?+

Every CCTV installation starts with a professional site survey. We map the space, identify critical zones, position cameras to reduce blind spots, and test before handover.

QIs your security equipment compatible with remote monitoring?+

Yes. Our surveillance and alarm systems support secure remote access through mobile and desktop applications.

QDo you offer security audits for existing setups?+

Yes. We conduct security audits for existing systems and provide a detailed gap analysis with practical recommendations.
