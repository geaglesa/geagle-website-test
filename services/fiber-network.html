FAQ

Common questions

QWhat is the difference between single-mode and multimode fiber?+

Single-mode fiber supports longer distances and higher bandwidth, making it ideal between buildings or across campuses. Multimode fiber is suited for shorter distances within a building. We recommend the right type based on your requirements.

QHow long does a fiber installation project take?+

A single-building installation typically takes 2 to 5 days depending on size and complexity. Larger multi-building or campus projects are planned in phases and can take several weeks.

QIs fiber optic cable affected by interference or weather?+

Fiber is immune to electromagnetic interference and electrical noise. Outdoor-rated and armored fiber cables are also designed to withstand heat, moisture, UV exposure, and environmental conditions.

QCan fiber be installed without disrupting our operations?+

In most cases, yes. We route cables carefully and schedule work that requires downtime during off-hours or weekends where possible, with clear communication throughout the project.

QDo you provide a warranty on fiber installations?+

Yes. Fiber installations are covered by a workmanship warranty, and we provide full test certification at handover. Ongoing maintenance contracts are also available.
