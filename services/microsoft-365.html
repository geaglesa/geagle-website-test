FAQ

Common questions

QWhich Microsoft 365 plan is right for our business?+

It depends on your size, budget, email needs, Teams usage, security requirements, and device management needs. We assess your environment and recommend the plan that delivers the best value.

QHow long does a Microsoft 365 migration take?+

A straightforward email migration for a small organization can take a few days. Larger migrations involving Exchange, SharePoint, and Teams typically take 2 to 6 weeks depending on volume and complexity.

QIs our data stored in Saudi Arabia or locally?+

Microsoft has data center regions in the UAE serving the Middle East. For specific data residency requirements, we advise on available options and configure your tenant around compliance obligations.

QWhat security features does Microsoft 365 include?+

Key capabilities include MFA, Conditional Access, Microsoft Defender for Office 365, Azure AD Identity Protection, DLP policies, and Microsoft Purview. We configure the relevant features as part of deployment.

QDo you provide support in Arabic?+

Yes. We provide Microsoft 365 support, training, and documentation in Arabic and English so users can get help in the language they prefer.

QWhat happens to our data if we stop using Microsoft 365?+

Before any transition away from Microsoft 365, we help export and migrate email, files, and other content to a new environment or archive with a clear data exit plan.
