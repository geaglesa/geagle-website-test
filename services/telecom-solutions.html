FAQ

Common questions

QWhat is the difference between MPLS and SD-WAN?+

MPLS is a traditional WAN technology using dedicated carrier circuits with predictable performance at higher cost. SD-WAN routes traffic intelligently across multiple lower-cost connections, often delivering flexibility and savings with careful planning.

QCan you help us reduce our telecom costs?+

Yes. We audit current services, identify unnecessary spend, renegotiate where appropriate, and redesign connectivity architecture to improve performance and lower cost.

QDo you work with all major telecom operators in Saudi Arabia?+

We work with multiple carriers and connectivity providers, including active project relationships with STC, Center 3, and Tata Communications.

QHow do you handle telecom projects across multiple sites in Saudi Arabia?+

We manage multi-site telecom projects centrally with carrier coordination, a dedicated project manager, disciplined reporting, and clear stakeholder communication.

QCan you replace our existing phone system with VOIP?+

Yes. We assess your current telephony environment, design a VOIP solution, manage migration, and provide ongoing support for a smooth transition.

QWhat redundancy options do you recommend for critical connectivity?+

At minimum, we recommend dual independent circuits from different providers with automatic failover through SD-WAN or router-based failover. For higher availability, we add 4G or 5G backup.
